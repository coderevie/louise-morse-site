About Louise Morse

What is channeling?

Channeling is the process of receiving and transmitting information or energy from a source beyond the physical realm. This can include communication with spirit guides, angels, or higher selves. The channeler acts as a conduit, allowing messages to flow through them, often during altered states of consciousness.
